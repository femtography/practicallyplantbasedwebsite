Plant Based Recipes

Plant Based Made Practical

Providing solutions to the two biggest obstacles when going plant based.

"What Would I Eat..?"

One of the most daunting tasks, when thinking of transitioning to a plant-based diet, is figuring out what you will be eating.

We have all been there! That is the reason that this recipe resource was created. We are here to provide you with the answer to this question so that you no longer have to entertain the idea that you are going to have to survive off of lettuce alone if you go plant-based.

It is our goal to show you that the majority of your favorite recipes can be modified to fit a plant-based diet. Often times you end up feeling that you are eating exceptionally delicious meals more frequently than ever.

So sit back and let us handle the details of what you will be eating, and focus on enjoying the delicious recipes that we have in store for you.

"Eating Healthy Is Expensive..."

Whether it’s organic, sustainable, or any other synonym for the healthy versions of food you imagine in a plant-based diet, it almost always is associated with paying more.

Going to a specialty “health foods” store is certainly going to up your grocery bill. What we found worked best was sticking to the grocery store’s brand, like Kroger, for the majority of my groceries and going to Trader Joe’s for the specialty items we love and can’t live without.

The best part of a plant-based diet is that all of our favorite protein items, such as beans and tofu, are incredibly affordable compared to the price of meat. Reducing your animal products will reduce the cost of your groceries, and you can be certain that we took that into account when making your recipes.

Jaim Benson

“ There’s a lot of reasons for going plant-based, whether it’s animal rights, environmental concerns, or just better health. I’ve been a vegetarian for three years now and, initially, my motivation for going plant-based was the conditions that animals face. It was not long before I realized that all of three of those reasons were important to me and I wanted to make a bigger change in the world, so I started improving all of those areas at once by going plant-based.

When I went plant-based, I was working full time on Active Duty and I was an amateur fighter, so I needed to keep my protein and nutrients up and my costs down. In all honesty, I relied heavily on faux meat products initially, because it’s hard to imagine a world where I can’t have chicken nuggets. I quickly learned to add beans, tofu, and extra vegetables to everything to make my meals more satisfying. Over time I found myself not even missing the meat and I never felt like I was missing out on protein or other essential nutrients, even when living an active lifestyle. Now, I’m in nursing school, so I’m busier than ever and my budget is as tight as ever. I live for cooking shortcuts and the idea of making delicious and healthy food without making everything from scratch. Who has the time..?

This website is a resource that provides you with a realistic option for plant-based meals that will be delicious, satisfying, and won’t be out of budget. I hope you take these recipes and create your own favorite plant-based dishes.“
